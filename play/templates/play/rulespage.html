GOAL

The goal of this game is to keep the pickaxe out of the lava for as many turns as possible.

Specifically, in single player mode your goal is to get a high score - this game is all about balancing risk.

TURNS

Each turn you have you two choices:

1. Move the pickaxe back to center square (you can only do this 5 times!)

2. Let the computer RANDOMLY move the pickaxe one of four directions (up, down, left or right)

If you are in the corner and you choose RANDOM and survive, you are awarded with +1 move for your bravery.

Good luck!!
